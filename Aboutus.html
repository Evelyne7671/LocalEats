Established in 2024, Local Eats began as a small, family-owned venture with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of Kenya'slocal dishes to the heart of Nairobi. Founded by Evelyne and Catherine, who were inspired by family traditions , the restaurant quickly became a local favorite. Over the years,Local Eats has grown from a modest eatery into a beloved gathering spot for both locals and visitors, known for its homemade recipes. The restaurant has continued to thrive by staying true to its roots while also embracing new culinary trends. Today, LocalEats remains a cherished fixture in the community, dedicated to providing exceptional dining experiences for everyone who walks through its doors
